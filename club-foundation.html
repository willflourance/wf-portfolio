Website Redesign

CMAA World Conference and Club Business Expo

Challenge + Opportunity

The Club Foundation (CF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to professional advancement and promoting growth within the private club industry. The Foundation actively fundraises to provide for scholarships, grants, research, and outreach initiatives with industry partners. The CF site was in pretty poor shape when I took on the project - an amalgam of competing content, accessibility issues, confusing navigation, and inconsistent identity. To rebuild the site effectively I had to know what was worth including and most importantly who I was including it for.

Process + Result

I identified the users and their motivations for accessing content on the site. The typical user visits to perform actions like applying for a scholarship or making a donation. Framing these tasks within content blocks and site navigation allowed for user flows that guide action at the user’s own pace, without overwhelming them with information. Within this simplified structure I applied a cohesive style library of colors, type and other graphic elements that convey brand messages with a sense of aspiration and goodwill.
