Website Redesign

CMAA World Conference and Club Business Expo

Challenge + Opportunity

The CMAA World Conference and Club Business Expo is the largest event for professionals in the Private Club Industry. Prior to my time at CMAA the event’s online presence was limited to a series of pages within the existing CMAA site. This was a source of confusion for many registrants since information was densely packed within the limitations of CMAA.org’s navigation and layout. In addition, the location of the conference was presented too prominently, selling the destination rather than the educational and professional experiences offered.

Process + Result

World Conference is such a multi-faceted event that it requires a free-standing website to present content with appropriate detail. Creating a dedicated site allowed the content of the event site to flow without crowding brand copy and fine print within the same spaces. This approach also allowed newfound flexibility in visual design. I was able to include photos and graphic elements to convey the experience of attending the event, conveying the vibrance of its programming.

For the 2019 World Conference I share oversight with a rebranding effort supplemented by third-party marketing agency MDG. I was instrumental in guiding their team to create assets and branding guidelines that amplified the event’s value while emphasizing member-first messaging. I asserted a creative strategy that values brand consistency from year to year with a clean and colorful presentation. The resulting output enabled me to launch the most web-friendly version of the World Conference site to date, and establish production standards to ensure continued quality of all future CMAA event sites.
