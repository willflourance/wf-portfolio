ABOUT ◣

I’m Will Flourance, a multidisciplinary designer from washington, DC

I have worked on projects in the industries of Private Club Management, Non-Profit Organizations, Real Estate, and Healthcare in both in-house and freelance settings. I create websites with emphasis on usability and brand appeal.

Great design doesn’t have to be Art. Art can be hard to understand and even harder to make! Great design should help you make sense of the world instead of making you go “hmmmm”. In addition great design should make new ideas and information accessible to all, not just those who are “tuned in”.

As a Designer it is my place to immerse myself in the subject matter so others don’t have to, and present ideas and concepts in clear and compelling ways.
